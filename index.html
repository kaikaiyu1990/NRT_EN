About Us

Company History：

NEU REALMS TECHNOLOGY was officially established in 2023 in Singapore. Our company was founded out of a deep belief in the potential of the gaming industry and an infinite love for technological innovation and gaming entertainment. Initially, we started as a small team focused on researching and developing innovative game concepts, as well as exploring the possibilities of different gaming platforms. Through firm determination and continuous efforts, we quickly expanded our team and achieved initial success in the game development and agency fields.

Collaboration team：

NEU REALMS TECHNOLOGY was founded due to our infinite passion for the world of games. We believe that games are not only entertainment, but also a power of connection and inspiration. We have team members from different backgrounds and professional fields around the world, including professionals from game development, art design, technical research, marketing and other fields, working together to create wonderful game products.

Company Vision：

Our goal is not only to pursue excellence in game development, but also to build a creative and inspiring game culture. We value diversity and inclusion and encourage team members from different cultural backgrounds and perspectives to reach their highest potential. The power of this diversity allows us to create inspiring and deep gaming experiences that players can find resonance and joy in.

Mission and Commitment：

NRT Gaming will look forward to becoming a creator and leader in the gaming world through continuous innovation, technological breakthroughs and the ultimate pursuit of quality. At the same time, we are committed to creating the best, most innovative, and most engaging game experiences in the global game industry, striving to establish an exciting and top-notch brand that connects people in the game industry, and through the charm of games, we will bring joy and challenge Bring it to players around the world and become their trustworthy and beloved gaming partner.
